# Gravity Engine · 引力引擎

A civilization-scale, bilingual (EN / 中文) exploration of gravity — from falling apples to curved spacetime, black holes, gravitational waves, dark matter, quantum gravity, and emergent information-theoretic models. The central thesis: gravity may not be a force at all, but the geometry of reality — and perhaps the deepest architecture of the universe itself.

Ten deep systems (history → Newton → relativity → black holes → gravitational waves → quantum gravity → cosmic structure & dark matter → entropy/emergent gravity → gravity technology → the future of gravity theory), a 7-axis "Gravity Structure" meta-model, and a recursive engine that runs gravity scale by scale from the quantum vacuum to civilization-scale spacetime engineering. Every simulation, equation, timeline and explanation switches language live, without reload.

Next.js 14 (App Router) · React 18 · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS. All visualizations are hand-built with HTML Canvas and SVG — no charting or 3-D dependencies. Bilingual state via a lightweight React context (`lang.tsx`).
